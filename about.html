Our Story

Ruby Pictures was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate photographers who shared a vision to make professional photography accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background or social status.

The idea was sparked when our founder, Ruby Dlamini, noticed a growing gap in the photography industry. High-quality photography was often viewed as a luxury, leaving many without the opportunity to capture their most important milestones. Ruby set out to change that.

From a small home studio in Johannesburg, Ruby Pictures evolved into a community-focused photography business. We built our reputation on inclusivity, affordability, and creativity, offering personalized packages for intimate family shoots, graduations, weddings, and corporate events.

At Ruby Pictures, we believe every story deserves to be told beautifully. Our team takes pride in working with people from all walks of life, ensuring every client feels comfortable and confident in front of the camera. Today, Ruby Pictures is a movement to democratize photography.

Core Values:

  • Affordability
  • Inclusivity
  • Creativity
